What is the role of small cities in the regional urban network, and what are their specific socio-spatial and morphological dynamics, which are often overlooked in urban studies?

As a member of the Mikripoli Network, I collaborated on multiple research projects, performing statistical and spatial data analysis - developing typologies and comparative case studies.

The work resulted in a robust body of publications that deepen the knowledge about small cities in Rio Grande do Sul, providing theoretical and practical subsidies for urban planning. 

**Data Sources:** IBGE, Google Earth, City Hall Data, OpenStreetMap, Field Research.

**Tools and Methods:** QGIS, Statistical Analysis, Morphological Analysis.

**Publications:**

  * **[Detoni, Faccin, Silveira, Rorato& Machado (2025)](https://www.rbgdr.net/revista/index.php/rbgdr/article/view/8020)** (en): Extreme climatic events and their socio-spatial impacts on small cities in Rio Grande do Sul.
  * **[Silveira, Faccin& Detoni (2023)](https://online.unisc.br/seer/index.php/redes/article/view/18531)** (en): Analysis of the role of small cities in changes within the regional urban network.
  * [**Silveira, Faccin, Detoni & Silva (2024)**](https://online.unisc.br/seer/index.php/redes/article/view/19899) (pt/br): Comparative analysis of the urban morphology of three small cities in Rio Grande do Sul.
  * **[Silveira _, Faccin, Detoni, Menezes & Haas _(2022)](https://revistas.planejamento.rs.gov.br/index.php/boletim-geografico-rs/article/view/4488)** (pt/br): Construction of a typology of small cities in Rio Grande do Sul.
  * **[Rorato, Detoni& Faccin (2022)](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/373975584_Cidades_pequenas_no_contexto_do_ensino_superior_relato_de_experiencia_da_disciplina_de_Sistemas_de_Informacoes_Geograficas_em_Urbanismo)** (pt/br): Experience report on studies about small cities in the GIS in Urbanism course at the UFRGS School of Architecture.
  * **[Detoni, Faccin& Rorato (2023)](https://periodicos.ufpel.edu.br/index.php/pixo/article/view/26495)** (pt/br): Report on the "Architectures of a Small City" collage workshop held at Sinapeq in 2022.
