I combine a background in urban studies with experience in data analysis to produce evidence that supports urban planning and the development of public policies.

My professional trajectory encompasses academic and applied experience, reflected in over 30 scientific publications and projects focused on cities, territory, climate, economy, urban vulnerability, and socio-spatial transformations.

I believe in teamwork for the collective construction of knowledge and, above all, in the power of data visualization, storytelling, and clear communication to improve decision-making processes.
